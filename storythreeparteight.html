Then, around 3 AM, I heard footsteps. They were coming back. It was him. This time, he wasn’t empty-handed. He was holding something. And that something… was an axe.

I froze. I had always heard that in moments of true fear, your body can lock up — paralyzed by shock. Now I understood. I couldn’t move. Could barely breathe. It felt like I’d been dropped into a scene from The Shining.
