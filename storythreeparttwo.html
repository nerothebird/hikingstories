I had packed the day before and set out around noon. In hindsight, starting in 30-degree heat wasn’t my brightest idea, but I welcomed the challenge. The hike started off well — aside from walking two kilometers in the wrong direction. Eventually I found the right trail, and by 4 PM I took a break, estimating I had about two hours left to go. I was right. At 5:50 PM, I arrived at the shelter.
