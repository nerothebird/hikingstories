But now, for a real one. I was hiking on the Laurel Highlands trail. It was day 2, a nice, sunny afternoon, and I planned on something like 17 miles. After the trail leaves Ohiopyle SP, it crosses a road and goes down an embankment into State Game Lands. It's a wetter, almost bottomland type forest. Mature canopy with a fern layer and shrubby hammocks where trees have fallen.
